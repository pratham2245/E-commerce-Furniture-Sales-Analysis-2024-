# E-commerce-Furniture-Sales-Analysis-2024-

<img width="258" height="234" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ad55e056-dd99-40b5-ab5f-15146127dc0b" />


This project analyzes online furniture sales data to understand product performance, pricing behavior, discount impact, and revenue concentration.  
The project follows a **complete data analyst workflow** with strong business relevance.

---

##  Objective
The objective of this project is to:
- Analyze revenue and demand patterns
- Identify top-performing products
- Study pricing and discount effectiveness
- Understand revenue concentration using Pareto analysis
- Support inventory and marketing optimization

---

##  Dataset Description
The dataset includes:
- Product Title  
- Original Price  
- Selling Price  
- Units Sold  
- Product Tags  

Each record represents an e-commerce furniture product.

---

##  Key Focus Areas
- Revenue and units sold analysis  
- Top 10 products by revenue  
- Product tag-wise performance  
- Price vs units sold relationship  
- Discount percentage impact  
- Pareto (80/20) revenue concentration  

---

##  Data Cleaning & Preparation
Key steps include:
- Standardizing column names  
- Converting price and sales columns to numeric  
- Removing duplicates and invalid records  
- Creating derived metrics such as revenue and discount percentage  

---

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Visualizations created include:
- Price, units sold, and revenue distributions  
- Top 10 products by revenue  
- Tag-wise revenue comparison  
- Price vs demand scatter plots  
- Pareto curve for revenue concentration  

---

##  Key Insights
- A small number of products generate most of the revenue  
- Lower-priced products sell more units  
- Discounts increase sales volume but reduce margins if overused  
- Certain product tags consistently outperform others  

---

##  Business Use & Impact
The analysis supports:
- Inventory prioritization  
- Pricing and discount optimization  
- Targeted marketing strategies  
- Revenue-focused decision-making  

---

##  Tools & Technologies Used
- Python  
- Pandas & NumPy  
- Matplotlib & Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how product-level sales data can be transformed into actionable business insights. By identifying revenue drivers and demand patterns, the analysis helps improve e-commerce retail strategy and operational efficiency.
